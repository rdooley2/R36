Solar Energy Consumption by Month

(Trillion Btu)

This data is from the Energy Information Administration (EIA). The graph above depicts US solar energy consumption from the first seven months of 2024. It is measured in Trillion British thermal units which shows how much energy is being consumed. With this in mind, the United States is on track to consume the highest amount of solar energy in recent years.

Source: Energy Information Administration (EIA)
