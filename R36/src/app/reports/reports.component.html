Solar Electricity Net Generation by Month

(Million Kilowatthours)

This data is from the Energy Information Administration (EIA). The graph above depicts solar energy generation from the first seven months of 2024. It is measured in Million Kilowatthours which shows how much energy is being created. This shows that we are on track to generate the highest amount of solar energy in recent years.
